“If I were asked to give what I consider
the single most useful bit of advice for all humanity,
it would be this:
Expect trouble as an inevitable part of life,
and when it comes, hold your head high.
Look it squarely in the eye, and say,
‘I will be bigger than you. You cannot defeat me.'”
~ Ann Landers

Ann Landers was a pen name created by Chicago Sun-Times advice columnist Ruth Crowley in 1943 and taken over by Eppie Lederer in 1955. For 56 years, the Ask Ann Landers syndicated advice column was a regular feature in many newspapers across North America… source
